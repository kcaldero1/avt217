Program

1) Open Adobe XD

2) Canvas size: Custom 1000 x 1000

3) Name canvas cake

4) Select Rectangle Tool

5) Bottom layer rectangle (W:614 and H:170) and color is ##A60B0B and no border

6) Middle layer is (W:452 and H:170) and color is ##DF1313 and no border

7) Top layer is (W:319 and H:170) and color is #F2A4A4 and no border

8) Layer all rectangles on top of another like the layers of a cake

9) All the layers should be in the middle of the artboard

10) Export your file
