About Pines of Nowhere

The Pines of Nowhere was established in 1998 with one mission: to provide guests with the Ultra-Fresh Seafood Experience™. Featuring seasonal and sustainable seafood that is flown in fresh daily, our chef-driven menu proves that no matter where you’re dining our seafood can be truly exceptional. Woven into everything that we do, our passion for top of the catch fish, fine wines and unforgettable service has been recognized time and time again. Some of our accolades include: WINE SPECTATOR’S Award of Excellence, TRIPADVISOR’S Certificate of Excellence Award for Superior Service, and Pines of Nowhere’ s Special Occasion Restaurant.
